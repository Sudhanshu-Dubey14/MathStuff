

# Q8 Use Runge-Kutta Method to find the value of y when x=0.5, given that y=1, when x=0 and that y'=(y-x)/(y+x).

## Answer : 

 Using 1st order R-K method : k = h*y'(x~n~,y~n~) & y~n+1~ = y~n~ + k

 We get : 

 n | X | Y
---|---|---
1 | 0.100000 | 1.100000
2 | 0.200000 | 1.200000
3 | 0.300000 | 1.300000
4 | 0.400000 | 1.400000
5 | 0.500000 | 1.500000


 Hence at x= 0.500000 , **y= 1.500000**.
